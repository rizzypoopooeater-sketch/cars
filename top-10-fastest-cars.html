Notes on the ranking.

This list ranks road-legal production cars by their highest achieved or officially rated top speed. Track-only builds (McMurtry Spéirling, Bugatti Bolide, Aston Martin Valkyrie AMR Pro) and one-off concepts are excluded. Speed figures are drawn from manufacturer claims and independent verification bodies including TÜV and Racelogic. Where runs are simulated or unverified, the "Simulated" or "Claimed" tag appears; where the number has been certified through instrumented testing, the "Verified" or "Record" tag appears.

Acceleration is a different game. Electric cars like the Rimac Nevera and Lucid Air Sapphire dominate 0–60 mph charts, and the McMurtry Spéirling holds the outright production acceleration crown (track-only). But on top speed — a one-dimensional fight against air resistance at enormous velocity — these ten cars are currently standing alone.
